These are some notes which were brainstormed at SPI's March 2019 in-person
board meeting that board members should consider when handling new associated
project inquiries. This is not a formal board policy or any other official
document, but we do want to get more systematic about this.

* Make sure they are a free software project
* Are substantial and significant (at least a few people involved, with at least different affiliation/employers; have a user base)
* Do they understand our restrictions (transfers outbound)
* Are they the rights holders?  (If employer owns code, etc)
* Outstanding legal threats
